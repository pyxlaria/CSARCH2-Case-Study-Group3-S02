# CSARCH2-Case-Study-Submission
Group Title: The Digital Arms Race: The Evolution of Cyberassault

Group Member Roster:
Ethan Yuric Lim, Mynt Tan, Ulrich Maeko Gonzales, Miguel Diego Loste, Ezekiel Tiangson

Group’s Topic Theme:
The group’s exhibit theme centers around the evolution of cyberassault. The exhibit will focus on the slow evolution of cyberassault and cybersecurity throughout the years. Rather than focus on the software aspect of each cyberassault (the specifics on how they breached security), the group plans to focus more on the impacts each cyberassault had on society and cybersecurity as a whole. 

Interactive Element
The interactive element would be a timeline with a quizzing system attached that the user can interact with and earn a score from. The timeline would show have a point on the line wherein each point would showcase the biggest cyberattack of that year. Bigger points would also be shown, showcasing major cyberattacks and/or cyberattacks that made significant impacts on modern security. Throughout the timeline, there would also be points where the user would be quizzed as to details of a certain cyberattack, the most common cyberattack, etc. A score would be recorded and be displayed at the end when the user has completed all the quizzes
